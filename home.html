SYNOPSIS

NullBourne is a sci-fi/fantasy adventure, action, and psychological thriller. The story opens up with a newly emerged spacefaring and cyberpunk civilization, amidst a time of great change, vast unknowns, and complex adversities. It follows a naturally expanding and ever-dynamic cast of individuals, all part of one massively interconnected journey and universe.



  In an era where tensions have spiked due to recent, seemingly impossible phenomena, uncertainty rises to the top. A sliver of the population has inexplicably emerged with exotic abilities and anomalous maladies. Man-eating aberrant humans known as Vores/Vorax lurk in the shadows of sprawling megacities. Criminal organizations and Faction uprisings spark fear and chaos throughout a solar system on the verge of new frontiers. New Year's Day marks the beginning...


  An undergrad research scientist with "photographic memory" catches unexpected attention from the Directive government. A police soldier from the CORE division starts his first unofficial day on the job. And last but not least, a criminal from the Base Belt District gets ready for a pivotal operation with his squad, years in the making.


  Discoveries and developments will be made, for better or for worse. Fortunes good and bad, alongside interim slices of life, will be consequential all throughout. Minds, abilities, truths, and lives will be contested. Stories will intertwine and clash, and amidst the grand scheme of things either come to be unraveled, or snuffed out. Ultimately individuals will come to realize, one way or another, that beyond our drifting iceberg that contains all that we know and don't know... Lies the most vast and boundless, ocean of unknown unknowns.
